NCERT Chapter Formulas

Tap any card to inspect variables and physical meanings.

Current Density

J = I / A = n e v_d

Vector quantity (A/m²). Points in direction of current.
Drift Velocity

v_d = (e E τ) / m

e=charge, E=Electric Field, τ=relaxation time, m=electron mass
Resistivity

ρ = m / (n e² τ)

ρ=Resistivity (Ω·m). Independent of conductor geometry.
Temp Dependence

ρ_T = ρ_0 [1 + α ΔT]

α = Temperature coefficient of resistivity (K⁻¹ or °C⁻¹).
Terminal EMF

V = ε - I r

ε=EMF, r=internal resistance, V=terminal potential difference.
Wheatstone Bridge

R1 / R2 = R3 / R4

Balance condition when zero current flows through galvanometer.
